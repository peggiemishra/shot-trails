Dear Visitor,

My name is Peggie Mishra, and I'm so glad you're here.

Photography has been a quiet companion throughout my journey. It has taught me to slow down, observe, and appreciate the beauty hidden in everyday moments. I love photographing nature, art, culture, and the simple scenes that often go unnoticed.

Over the past decade, my photographs have travelled far beyond my camera. They have been featured by magazines, research organizations, educational institutions, travel platforms, and publications around the world.

This website is a small archive of that journey—a place where every photograph carries not only a memory, but also the stories it continued to tell after it was captured.

I hope you enjoy exploring this collection as much as I enjoyed creating it.

With gratitude and love,

Peggie Mishra
