Dear Visitor,

I am Peggie Mishra.

My love for photography has taken my work to places I never imagined, and this little corner of the internet is a celebration of that journey.

I enjoy photographing nature, art, culture, and the beautiful everyday moments around me. I believe that even the ordinary holds a story worth preserving, and photography has always been my way of capturing those fleeting moments in time.

Over the years, many of these photographs have found a place in magazines, research publications, educational institutions, travel platforms, and digital stories across the world. It is both humbling and deeply rewarding to know that these images have helped tell stories far beyond the moment they were captured.

I am incredibly grateful to every publication, editor, researcher, writer, and organization that chose to feature my work. Your support and appreciation have made this journey even more meaningful.

This website is a collection of those moments—a place where you can explore not only the photographs themselves but also the paths they have travelled over more than a decade.

Thank you for visiting, and I hope you enjoy this journey as much as I have enjoyed living it.

With gratitude and love,

Peggie Mishra
