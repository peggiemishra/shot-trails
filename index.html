Dear Visitor,

I am Peggie Mishra. My love for photography is all about capturing nature, art, culture, and the ordinary everyday moments that hold a story worth preserving.

Over the past decade, it has been deeply rewarding to see these images travel beyond the shutter—finding a home in magazines, research journals, educational platforms, and travel stories worldwide.

This space is a collection of those visual journeys. I’m incredibly grateful to every editor, researcher, and writer who featured my work, and to you for visiting. I hope you enjoy exploring these footprints!

With gratitude and love ❤️ 🌸,
